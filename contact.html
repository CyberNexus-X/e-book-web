Frequently Asked Questions

How do I pay for Skill Library?

You pay a one-time ₹149 through our secure Superprofile checkout, which supports UPI, debit/credit cards, and net banking. There are no subscriptions or recurring charges.

When and how is my library delivered?

After a successful payment, a secure Google Drive access link is emailed to you. Delivery is usually instant and always within 24 hours. If it doesn't arrive, contact support and we'll resend it.

What does lifetime access include?

Lifetime access means your purchased Google Drive library stays available for as long as Skill Library runs the service. You can read or download the eBooks anytime on your own devices.

Can I download the eBooks to read offline?

Yes. The books are in PDF/EPUB formats, so you can download them to your phone, tablet, computer, or e-reader and read offline whenever you like.

My Google Drive link isn't opening. What do I do?

First try opening it in a different browser or after signing into your Google account. If it still fails, message support with your order reference and we'll provide an alternative access method.

How do I reach customer support?

Email support@skilllibrary.shop, message us on Instagram @skill_library.in, or use the Telegram link above. We reply within 24 hours.

Will the library get new updates or books?

We continuously maintain and improve the collection. While we don't guarantee specific new titles, we work to keep the library organized, accessible, and valuable over time.

Can I get a refund?

Because Skill Library is a digital product delivered electronically, refunds are limited. Eligible cases include duplicate or system-error payments and failed delivery. See our Refund Policy for full details.
