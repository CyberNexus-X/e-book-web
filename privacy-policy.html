Privacy Policy

We keep things simple and honest. Here's exactly what we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it.

Your data is safe with us
Last Updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Skill Library ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit our website or purchase our digital eBook bundle. By using our website, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

We take your privacy seriously. We don't sell your data. We don't spam you. And we collect only what's necessary to complete your order and deliver your library access.

1. Information We Collect

When you visit our website or make a purchase, we may collect the following types of information:

Personal Information

  • Name — Provided voluntarily when you reach out to us or fill in purchase details
  • Email address — Used to deliver your Google Drive library link after payment
  • Phone number — Collected by our payment processor if required for payment verification

Technical Information

  • IP address and browser type (collected automatically through web analytics)
  • Pages visited, time spent on site, and referring URLs
  • Device type and operating system

We do not collect government IDs, financial account details, passwords, or any sensitive personal data directly on our website.

2. Payment Information

All payments are processed through Superprofile, our third-party payment partner. Superprofile handles your payment details — including UPI, debit/credit card, and net banking — through their secure, PCI-compliant infrastructure.

Skill Library does not store, see, or have access to your card numbers, UPI PINs, or banking credentials. Payment data is entirely handled by Superprofile under their own privacy and security standards.

After a successful payment, Superprofile sends us a notification confirming your email address and order completion. We use this information solely to deliver your Google Drive library link.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes only:

  • To deliver your purchased eBook library link via email after payment confirmation
  • To respond to your support queries or questions sent through our contact channels
  • To analyze website traffic and improve user experience using analytics tools
  • To detect and prevent fraudulent transactions or misuse of our service
  • To send important service-related notices (such as delivery confirmations)

We do not use your email for marketing campaigns unless you have explicitly opted in.

4. Cookies and Tracking

Our website may use cookies — small text files stored on your browser — to improve site functionality and analyze traffic patterns. These include:

Essential Cookies

These are necessary for the website to function correctly. They do not collect personal information and cannot be turned off.

Analytics Cookies

We may use Google Analytics or similar tools to understand how visitors use our website. This data is anonymized and aggregated — we cannot identify individual users from this data.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time. This may affect some website functionality but will not prevent you from accessing your purchased content.

5. Third-Party Services

We work with a small number of trusted third parties to run our service:

  • Superprofile — Payment processing and order management
  • Google Drive — Delivery of your purchased eBook library
  • Google Analytics — Website traffic analysis (anonymized)

Each of these services has their own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them if you'd like to understand how they handle data.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with advertisers or unrelated third parties.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. Our website uses HTTPS encryption for all data in transit.

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security. We encourage you to avoid sharing sensitive details over unencrypted channels.

7. Email Communication

After your purchase, we send you one transactional email containing your Google Drive library link. We may occasionally send service-related updates, but we are not a marketing email platform.

If you contact us via email or social media, we will use the contact information you provide to respond to your query. We will not add you to any mailing lists without your explicit consent.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of any inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations)
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with the appropriate data protection authority in your jurisdiction

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at our Contact page. We will respond within 7 working days.

9. Children's Privacy

Skill Library is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to delete it.

10. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. When we make changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

We recommend checking this page periodically. Continued use of our website after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your information is handled, please reach out to us:

  • Email: support@skilllibrary.shop
  • Instagram: @skill_library.in
  • Contact Form: Visit our Contact page

We'll do our best to address any privacy concerns you have as quickly as possible.
